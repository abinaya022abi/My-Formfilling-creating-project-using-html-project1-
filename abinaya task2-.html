The Brihadeeswarar Temple

built around 1010 AD by the Chola emperor Rajaraja 1

Brihadeeswarar Temple in Thanjavur was built by Raja Raja Chola I in 1010 AD. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, it's famous for its tall tower and grand Dravidian architecture.

Sri ranganathaswamy temple

boasts a rich history spanning over a thousand years

The Sri Ranganathaswamy Temple in Srirangam is a famous Vishnu temple built by ancient kings. It is the largest functioning Hindu temple and a major pilgrimage site.
